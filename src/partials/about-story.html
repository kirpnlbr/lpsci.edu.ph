Brief History

Las Piñas City National Science High School is located at Carnival Park BF Resort Village. Originally, the proposal for the opening of a science high school in Las Piñas City was embodied in House Bill No. 5428 sponsored by Representative Cynthia A. Villar, Eduardo Reyes, and Rolando Andaya enacted on September 9, 2003 by the Senate and the House of Representatives as per committee report no. 976.

On February 19, 2007, during the Third Special Session of the 13th congress of the Philippines, Republic Act No. 9451 finally created Las Piñas City National Science High School.

With the birth of Las Piñas City National Science High School, with the active partnership of the Hon. Cynthia A. Villar and the DepEd Las Piñas Superintendent of City Schools, Dr. Lourdes M. Victoriano, preparation and plans for the curriculum, set-up and admission into the schools were made.
